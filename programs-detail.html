Banking

Credit Risk Assessment & Management

This course supports banking professionals to understand borrower risk, assess repayment capacity, structure credit facilities, and manage a healthy loan portfolio aligned with RMA guidelines.

⏱ 8 hours 📹 Live Zoom sessions 📚 Google Classroom 🎓 Digital Certificate 📼 Recorded Playback

What you will learn

  • How to assess borrower financial strength using qualitative and quantitative methods
  • How to identify early warning signs of credit deterioration and default risk
  • How to structure loans responsibly with appropriate collateral and covenants
  • How to manage portfolio risk, NPL ratios, and credit reporting obligations
  • How RMA prudential standards apply to credit risk governance

Who should attend

Credit officers, relationship managers, branch managers, and any banking professional involved in lending decisions or portfolio oversight. Suitable for both entry-level and experienced practitioners.

Delivery method

📹 Live sessions: 4 × 2-hour live Zoom workshops with Q&A
📚 Course materials: Slides, case studies, and readings on Google Classroom
📼 Recordings: All sessions recorded for 90-day playback access
✅ Assessment: 30-question online quiz (pass mark: 70%)
🎓 Certificate: FITI digital certificate issued on completion

About the instructor

Courses are facilitated by FITI-certified practitioners with active experience in Bhutan's financial sector. Guest sessions by RMA officers and senior banking professionals are included where available.

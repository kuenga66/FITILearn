About FITI

Bhutan's only dedicated training institute for the banking and insurance sector — building financial expertise since our founding.

Our Mission

Dedicated Solely to Financial Sector Excellence

The Financial Institutions Training Institute (FITI) exists with a singular purpose: to deliver world-class capacity-building programs exclusively for professionals in Bhutan's banking and insurance industries.

Unlike generic training providers, every program FITI offers is purpose-designed for the financial sector — grounded in real regulatory requirements, practical challenges, and the unique context of Bhutan's financial landscape.

Our Journey

FITI Milestones

From a vision to Bhutan's only specialized financial training institute — our story of growth and impact.

Est.
Founding
FITI Established

The Financial Institutions Training Institute was founded with a mandate to provide specialized training exclusively for Bhutan's banking and insurance sectors, addressing a critical gap in professional development.

Grow
Expansion
First 100 Graduates

FITI celebrated its first major milestone with 100 graduates completing certification programs across banking fundamentals, risk management, and insurance principles.

Part
Partnerships
15+ Institutional Partners

FITI established formal partnerships with all major banks and insurance companies in Bhutan, securing a stable client base and embedding FITI credentials into professional advancement pathways.

2025
2025
Reaching Break-Even

After years of investment in capacity and curriculum, FITI achieved financial break-even in early 2025 — a landmark in the institute's journey toward long-term sustainability.

FITI launches its digital platform and expanded program portfolio, including Digital Finance & Fintech and Leadership for Financial Managers — responding to the evolving needs of the sector.

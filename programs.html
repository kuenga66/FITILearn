Enrollment Process

How to Enroll

Getting started with FITI is simple. Follow these four steps to secure your place.

1
Create Account

Register on the FITI portal with your professional details and institutional affiliation.

2
Choose Program

Browse available programs and select those that match your career development goals.

3
Submit Application

Complete the enrollment form. Your institution may need to confirm your nomination.

4
Attend & Certify

Attend the program and receive your FITI certificate recognized across the financial sector.
